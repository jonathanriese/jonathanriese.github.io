Jean-Pierre Timbaud – Lycée Jean-Pierre Timbaud

Rue Jean-Pierre Timbaud

Jean-Pierre Timbaud (1904-1945) was a trained metal worker secretary of the French communist trade union CGT. He joined the French communist resistance during the German occupation during the Second World War. In October 1941, he was one of 26 hostages that were shot by the German occupiers in an internment camp in retaliation for the murder of a German officer.
